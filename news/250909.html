circa by audio damage is a looping-layering plugin akin to mlr (and continued evolutions like mlre). it's perhaps the first commercial plugin to directly support monome grids and arc. also, audio damage is one of the few audio companies offering linux support: circa is a good candidate for turning some old laptop into a standalone instrument.
