may 2026 marks twenty years since monome shipped out the first grids around the world. across those decades many things changed yet we're also kept to our initial vision of staying small, open, and curious.

here are some moments.

soundtrack suggestion (html player)

october 2004, val verde — one of the few handwired prototypes, mostly salvage components, daringly/unadvisably fragile.

january 2005, los angeles — so many of us insisted on playing on the floor, even though the invention of flash photography revealed what a filthy proposition this proved to be.

august 2005, pasadena — our first ever circuit board promised that some day the wires maybe wouldn't fall out during a show. the wire debugging flurry was spectacular theatre but we could see it wasn't a sustainable career path.

december 2007, philadelphia — evidence of the thorough integration of work, art, and life.

august 2017, delhi — the workshop left the living room and dining table, accumulating artifacts in the barn.

february 2013, delhi — workshop crime scene documentation, whereby table tennis was willfully disrepected. records indicate this may have been a yardsale inventory, given the presence of rare drops. the dignity of the sport was later restored.

october 2013, delhi — kelli and trent demonstrating high level soldering factory labor. we briefly rented space above the future luck dragon during the aleph days. ideas were forming.

march 2022, delhi — the same building, bottom floor destroyed and reimagined by our own hands. shown is the future location of the semi-public workshop with optimistic visions of devirtualization, all inside of an art store.

july 2025 — beechi insists that we reuse these cardboard boxes until they absolutely disintegrate, some of which must be a decade old by now. having less enthusiasm for cardboard, lemmy stayed home this day.

december 2024 — a cursed object is also an enchanted object. each arc produced between 2012 and 2022 had its fancy custom bearings annointed with industrial grease using a paintbrush from this object. sometimes we'd need to scoop more from the vat using that bit of circuit board cutoff. some habits are accidental don't get reevaluated for years because they just sortof work.

december 2025 — luck dragon is an idea and also an art store. imagination and curiosity can save us from the swamp of sadness.

november 2019 — softbits show rehearsal on the third floor of half hidden, summoning the spirits of victorian wallpaper and repurposed modern construction materials and costumes. weather, the year's halloween theme. we have a deep regard for weather and also halloween. if you agree we could probably be friends.

october 2025 — forlorn barn workshop later became studio. building a good studio requires only two steps. first, find a tree with irresistible energy. and then, be near.

october 2023 — the open side of the barn we succeeded in preventing the inevitable-seeming condition of over-accumulation, staying clear for performances and gatherings, except during harvest season when garlic and onions and potatos and shallots and herbs of all kinds hang around allover the place curing. most of my favorite music has happened here. eifel agrees.

july 2011 — built the same year as dublab alongside those first broadcasts, and later succumbed at the hands (feet) of friends of dublab in the barn. it was a good party. music and friends and fire.

october 2022 — endings and renewal.

august 2025 — rainbows from dirt.

june 2023 — a bright circle of time, shaped like a square, still here, new and the same.



thank you for coming on this journey with us. we continue.
